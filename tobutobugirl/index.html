--- redirect_from: /games/tobutobugirl --- Tobu Tobu Girl

Your cat is floating into the atmosphere and you are the only one who can save it. Jump, dash, and flap your arms to maneuver around perilous obstacles racing against the clock to retrieve your pet friend before it is to late!

Tobu Tobu Girl is a fun and challenging arcade platformer developed by Tangram Games featuring an original soundtrack by potato-tan.

Tobu Tobu Girl is scheduled to release for the Nintendo Game Boy on December 3rd 2017 as a free downloadable ROM and as a limited edition complete in box.

The game

Land on enemies to bounce off of their heads gaining height. Be careful! Some enemies are less susceptible to bouncing than others.

Use your dash to quickly maneuver around. Keep your dash count in mind. You can only dash three times. Land on an enemy to regain dashes.

Flap your arms to quickly travel upwards. Be careful! You can only boost for so long before running out of energy.

Dash down into enemies to propel yourself upwards while sending them crashing towards the ground. Do this to regain some of your boosting energy.

Physical release

Tobu Tobu Girl will release as a limited edition Game Boy cartridge. The package comes with a full-color Japanese style cardboard box and a full-color 12 page manual. The first batch will be limited to 75 copies with a second batch to follow soon after.
