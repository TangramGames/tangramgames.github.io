--- redirect_from: /games/tobutobugirldx/ --- Tobu Tobu Girl Deluxe

Tobu Tobu Girl Deluxe is an enhanced version of the original featuring improved, full-color graphics and will be fully compatible with both the Game Boy and Game Boy Color.

Like the original, this Deluxe version will also be open source and completely free to download upon release.

We are currently running a Kickstarter campaign in collaboration with publisher First Press Games to give Tobu Tobu Girl Deluxe a high-quality physical release. You can read more and pre-order a copy to help us reach our goal here.
